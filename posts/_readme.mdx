grew up in Ho Chi Minh City, Vietnam. In 2018, I moved to the US to pursue
a degree in Computer Science. I recently graduated from the University of
Maryland, College Park with a B.S. in Computer Science and a minor in
Statistics. I'm currently a SWE based in the Washington DC area.

for productivity's sake. As for keyboards, for those interested, I primarily use
my laptop's keybaord, and a [HHKB][hhkb] when I'm at home.
